Factors That Lead to Strokes


Uncontrollable Factors

  • Gender
  • Age
  • Family History

Controllable Factors

  • Hypertension: High Blood Pressure
  • Glucose Levels: Diabetes
  • Lack of Exercise
  • Obesity
  • Smoking

Stroke Prevention


What can you do to stay stroke-free?

  • Know and control your blood pressure!
  • Eat a healthy diet.
  • Become more active.
  • Lose weight if needed.
  • STOP SMOKING!!!
